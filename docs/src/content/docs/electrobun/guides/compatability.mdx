## Platform Support

### Development Platform
              <ul>
                <li><strong>macOS</strong>: Required for building Electrobun apps (Intel and Apple Silicon supported)</li>
                <li><strong>Windows</strong>: Development support available</li>
                <li><strong>Linux</strong>: Development support available</li>
              </ul>
