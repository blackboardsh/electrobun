Troubleshooting

If shortcuts don't trigger, check:

  • Accessibility permissions - On macOS, the app may need Accessibility access in System Preferences > Privacy & Security
  • Conflicting shortcuts - System or other apps may already use the same combination
  • VM environment - Running in a VM? Global shortcuts may be captured by the host OS
  • Focus - Some shortcuts may require a specific app focus state
